GOBI MANCHURIAN RECIPE

Manchurian is a class of Indian Chinese dishes made by roughly chopping and deep-frying ingredients such as chicken, cauliflower, prawns, fish, mutton, and paneer, and then saut√©eing them in a sauce flavored with soy sauce.

  • califlower
  • sauces
  • salt
